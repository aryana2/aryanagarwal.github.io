My name is Aryan Agarwal and I am a Master of Science student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, San Diego graduating in December 2023 with a focus in Medical Devices and Systems. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Engineering from the University of California, Irvine in June 2022. With a specialized background in biophotonics and business development, I am interested and enthusiastic about innovating healthcare technologies. For the past 3 years, my involvement in the medical device industry ranges from wearable mobile health sensors, computational modeling in tissue engineering, optical product development and entrepreneurship in ophthamology, team project management, algorithm and electronic design, and more. My professional and academic experiences have resulted in various entrepreneurship awards, research grants, and national honors.

As a detail-oriented and highly motivated engineer with a willingness to learn, I am eagerly seeking opportunities to work in a fast-paced environment to use my engineering skills to contribute to the advancement of patient healthcare.
